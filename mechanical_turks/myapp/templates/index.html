Text Incoherence

The purpose of this study is to assess text-incoherence in automatically generated text. We define text-incoherence as text which does not follow logically. Put another way, incoherent texts should not make clear sense. For example, "I'm hungry because it is snowing" should be labelled incoherent.

Each question in the form will include two texts and you are tasked with selecting the most incoherent text from the pair. In a follow-up question, you will be asked to indicate your confidence in your selection of the incoherent sentence, indicating how incoherent the selected text is relative to the non-selected sentence.
